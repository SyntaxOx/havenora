Our Story

The Havenora Experience

Havenora is derived from "haven," a place of comfort and belonging, and "ora," meaning moment or time.

We believe every event is a sanctuary in time—a carefully crafted space where meaningful connections flourish and lasting memories are born. Our approach combines Filipino hospitality with contemporary elegance, ensuring each gathering reflects your unique story.

Located in the heart of Antipolo, Rizal, we bring warmth, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail to every celebration we orchestrate.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide thoughtfully organized events rooted in Filipino ideals of hospitality, bayanihan, and malasakit. Through creativity and heartfelt service, we craft experiences that honor every moment and foster meaningful connections.

Our Vision

To become a leading MICE events company that embodies Filipino hospitality, creating warm environments where people feel appreciated and at home. Every event becomes a sanctuary of memorable moments and cultural pride.
